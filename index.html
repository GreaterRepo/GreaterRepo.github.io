About the Forum

The Bende Town Investment Forum is a platform dedicated to promoting sustainable investments in Bende Town and surrounding areas in Abia State. With a focus on economic development, we connect investors, entrepreneurs, and local stakeholders to foster innovation in agriculture, real estate, tourism, and infrastructure. Inspired by Abia's growing business environment under leaders like Governor Alex Otti, we aim to create jobs, empower communities, and drive long-term prosperity.

Investment Opportunities

  • Agriculture: Invest in palm oil, cassava, and livestock farming with high yields in Bende's fertile lands.
  • Real Estate: Develop residential and commercial properties in this emerging hub.
  • Tourism: Explore eco-tourism and cultural sites for hospitality ventures.
  • Infrastructure: Partner in roads, energy, and tech projects to support regional growth.
